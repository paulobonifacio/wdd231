Site Plan: Quebec Experience

Site Name

Quebec Experience

This name reflects the main goal of the site: to offer a rich and memorable experience to tourists exploring the city of Quebec.

Site Objective

The Quebec Experience website is dedicated to showcasing the wonders of Quebec City. It highlights iconic locations, culture, gastronomy, events, and provides practical information for visitors.

This site helps users plan their trip with advice on the best season to visit, climate, local currency, and much more.

Scenarios

  1. What are the main tourist attractions in Quebec?

    This scenario helps create content about iconic places such as Château Frontenac, Old Quebec, and events like the Quebec Winter Carnival.

  2. What are the best gastronomic experiences in Quebec?

    This scenario highlights local cuisine, such as the famous poutine, tourtières, and maple syrup.

Color Scheme

The color palette reflects the essence of Quebec:

  • Royal Blue (hsla(225, 70%, 40%, 1)): Used for titles and highlight elements.
  • Snow White (hsla(0, 100%, 99%, 1)): Used as the background color for a clean and pleasant visual experience.

Typography

  • Playfair Display (serif): Used for titles (h1, h2, h3) to reflect an elegant and classic style.
  • Roboto (sans-serif): Used for main text and paragraphs, ensuring optimal readability.

Wireframe

The wireframes of the different pages of the site will be developed to include intuitive navigation and clear sections, as follows:

  • Attractions Page
